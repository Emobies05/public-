Privacy Policy

Emowall AI 2.0 — Your Family's Guardian

Updated: March 2026

Data We Collect

Emowall AI collects the following data to provide safety features:

• Location data — For Guardian Mode GPS tracking and safe zone alerts

• Audio/Voice data — For voice commands, baby cry detection, and ragging detection. Audio is processed on-device and not stored on servers.

• Emergency contacts — Phone numbers you provide for SOS alerts

• Health tracking data — Women's health and child mood data stored locally on your device only

• Accelerometer data — For fall detection and accident detection

How We Use Data

• Location is used only for real-time safety features (GPS tracking, safe zones)

• Audio is processed locally — we do not record or store audio on servers

• Emergency contacts are used only to send SOS alerts when you activate them

• Health data stays on your device — never uploaded to servers

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. Emergency alerts are sent only to contacts you have chosen. We use Google services (Firebase, Maps) subject to Google's privacy policy.

Permissions

Emowall requests the following permissions:

• Location — Guardian Mode GPS

• Microphone — Voice commands and sound detection

• Camera — Evidence recording (Shield Mode)

• Contacts/Phone — Emergency SOS calls

• Notifications — Safety alerts

Children's Privacy

Emowall is designed to protect children. We do not collect personal data from children. All child-related features (Child Doctor AI, Digital Amma) process data locally on the device.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Emergency contact numbers are stored securely on your device.
