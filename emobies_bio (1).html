How It Works

Three steps. Door to door. That simple.

01
📝

Register Your Complaint

Open the Emobies app, describe what's wrong with your phone — cracked screen, dead battery, water damage, anything. Takes 60 seconds. No technical knowledge needed.

02
🛵

We Pick Up From Your Door

A verified Emobies delivery boy comes to your home or office. You hand over your phone. Track him in real time on the map — just like tracking a food delivery.

03
🔧

Expert Repair at Service Center

Your phone goes to one of 100+ verified service centers in Ernakulam. You can chat with the technician, track repair progress live, and get notified at every step.

04
🏠

Delivered Back — Fixed & Happy

Your repaired phone comes back to your door. You pay on delivery. And you earn EmoCoins automatically — 10 coins per repair, redeemable on future services.

Features

Everything your phone needs. Everything you deserve.

📍

Live Repair Tracking

Track your phone from pickup to repair to delivery on a live map — exactly like tracking a Swiggy order, but for your most valuable device. You always know where your phone is and what's being done to it.

💬

Direct Chat with Service Center

Talk directly to the technician working on your phone. Ask about parts, get repair estimates, confirm what needs to be replaced. No middleman, no guessing, no surprises on the bill.

✅

100+ Verified Service Centers

Every service center on the Emobies network is verified and rated. Covers all major brands — Samsung, iPhone, OnePlus, Vivo, Oppo, Realme, Xiaomi, and more. Ernakulam-wide coverage on Day 1.

⚡

Instant Payment to Everyone

The moment your phone is delivered back to you, payments split automatically through Razorpay — delivery boy gets his share, service center gets theirs, platform takes its commission. Instant. Transparent. Fair.

🔒

Secure Complaint System

Every complaint is logged, timestamped, and tracked with a unique ID. Super admin oversight, delivery boy accountability, and full repair history stored safely so you always have a record of what was done.

🤖

AI-Powered Customer Support

Powered by Gemini AI, Emobies support can answer questions, help you diagnose issues, guide you through the repair process, and escalate to human support instantly when needed.
